English

1. Managing a new language is always difficult, don't get ahead of yourself! go step step by step, remember to lay down your foundations well so that you can build on them, understand basics before moving on to intermediate or advanced. 2. Include English in activities that you enjoy, it is very possible, for example: - Watch series or movies with audio and subtitles in English - Do karaoke in English of your favorite songs - Read your favorite book in English

Learning

Learning has to be something you enjoy, so if you're not enjoying something is wrong. These are some tips when learning virtually. 1. Learn to teach and teach to learn, it is scientifically proven that if someone share the knowledge learned with someone else will be much less likely to I forgot them. 2. Everything has its time, if you are in college probably for every credit I know that he will ask for a certain time for you to dedicate to him, try to separate that time As much as you can do in less, use the remaining time to review the topics.
